Meetup

Developers embracing the serverless paradigm leveraging existing PaaS / SaaS solutions for building new digital services. By leveraging PaaS / SaaS, the developer does not need to take servers into account and can focus his/her efforts on actual service logic. Serverless.fi organizes events for exchanging experiences and spreading this knowledge.
